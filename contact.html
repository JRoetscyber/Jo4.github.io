Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you cover for coffee services?

JO4 Brew serves the greater Cape Town area and surrounding regions including Stellenbosch, Franschhoek, Paarl, and Somerset West. For locations further afield, additional travel fees may apply.

Do you offer remote cybersecurity consulting?

Yes, JO4 SECURE offers remote cybersecurity consulting, penetration testing, and code reviews for clients worldwide. We use secure communication channels and can work with your team across different time zones.

What real estate services do you provide?

As a certified property practitioner, I offer comprehensive real estate services including property sales, purchases, rentals, and valuations. I specialize in residential properties in the Cape Town area and work in partnership with Larney Properties.

How far in advance should I book coffee services for my event?

For weddings and large events, I recommend booking 3-6 months in advance as dates fill up quickly, especially during the peak season (October-April). For smaller events, 4-6 weeks notice is typically sufficient, but earlier is always better to ensure availability.

What is your typical response time?

I aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent security matters, please use the emergency contact number provided in the security services section.
