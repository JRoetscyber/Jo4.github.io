I'm Jonathan Roets, a cybersecurity professional, coffee entrepreneur, and real estate consultant based in Nigel, South Africa.

My journey began in the realm of information security, where I developed a passion for protecting digital assets and infrastructure from evolving cyber threats. Over the years, I've expanded my expertise into diverse fields, founding JO4 as an umbrella for my professional ventures.

Through JO4 Security, I provide comprehensive cybersecurity services including penetration testing, vulnerability assessments, and security consulting. My mobile coffee business, JO4 Brew, combines my love for premium coffee with entrepreneurial spirit, delivering exceptional coffee experiences at events and gatherings. In the real estate sector, I help clients navigate property transactions with expert guidance and market insights.
